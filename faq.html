Frequently Asked Questions

Will the drinks stay hot/ cold?

Temperature consistency is our forte. As is speedy delivery. All to ensure you the freshest, most pitch-perfect beverage possible.

Why Chai Dog?

Aside from our irresistible selection and fun mascot, coffee and tea are a great way to show staff appreciation. Chai Dog offers has been proven by a lab full of experts to increase productivity, and save time in repetitive trips to cafes and collecting group orders. We handle the details, you focus on your business and the delicious, exciting reward.

How long is delivery time?

Recurring and scheduled orders can be delivered at the precise moment of your request. Orders placed on the spot will be delivered within 25 minutes.

My office has a lot of dietary restrictions. Do you have nutrition information?

Absolutely! Details are listed on each menu item, including ingredients, nutritional information. Please reach out if you have any specific concerns, in the notes section of your order, or contact us directly.
